The answer to your glutinous desires lies in kale chips! I'm going to give you a really simple recipe that gives you all that crunchy goodness in a green leaf that will keep you from getting a heart attack.

Ingredients

Here's everything you need. Seriously, it's a short list. As you purchase these items, click on them to remove them from the list!

  • 1 bunch kale
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • A pinch of salt

Directions

Preparation

The prep is a very simple process.

The first thing you want to do is remove the stalk from the leaves

After that remove wash the kale and put it through the salad spinner to dry it thoroughly

The next step is to put the kale in a bowl and toss it with the olive oil

Place the kale in a baking tray and sprinkle salt on top

Cooking Instructions

The cooking itself is very simple. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and place the baking tray into the oven. Bake for 10 minutes and flip the kale. Bake for another 10 minutes or until kale is crispy. Take the baking tray out and let it cool for 2 to 3 minutes. Voila, you've got a healthy crunchy snack!
